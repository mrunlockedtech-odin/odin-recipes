Garlic Bread

Description

This recipe will take you through the steps of creating a very simple garlic bread with only 3 ingredients. Although pictured, cheese will not be present in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Any Type of Bread (I prefer a french baguette from Aldi)
  • Butter/Butter Spray/Margarine

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees farenheit
  2. Cut the baguette in half horizontally
  3. Generously butter the inner side of each baguette half
  4. Peel and finely dice your garlic (I prefer roughly 1 clove per half
  5. Spread the garlic evenly on each bread half then place in the oven
  6. While cook time various from bread to bread, I prefer a light toast of about 4-6 minutes
