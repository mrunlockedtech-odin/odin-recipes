Noodles

Description

This dish is a very simple "ramen" that is cooked in the microwave for a very quick meal.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Bowl
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Ramen Spice Package

Steps

  1. Tear open ramen package to extract the noodles themselves and the spice package
  2. Place the noodles in a bowl and cover with water
  3. Use the microwave to heat the contents for 3 minutes on high heat
  4. Carefully remove the bowl from the microwave and add the spice package
  5. Mix the contents for roughly 1 minute and enjoy
